Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the GlyphGuard — Scam & Phishing Detector mobile app, provided by Voldirex. GlyphGuard is built around offline-first analysis: the main character and text safety checks run on your device.

Overview

GlyphGuard helps you inspect text, links, QR content, and similar inputs for suspicious look-alike characters, phishing patterns, and known threat indicators.

We do not require you to create an account to use the app. We do not sell personal data. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs inside the app.

What Stays on Your Device

Core analysis is designed to run locally on your device. This includes the character-level checks used to identify homoglyph and look-alike substitutions in text and links.

  • Text you paste into the app for local analysis stays on your device unless you explicitly use an online check.
  • Local history, preferences, and cached threat data are stored on your device and can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • We do not use your local analysis content to build advertising profiles.

What May Be Sent Over the Network

Some app features use network services. When you choose or trigger those features, limited data may be transmitted to our backend or infrastructure providers.

  • Threat database update requests may send standard technical request metadata such as IP address, app version, and basic delivery information needed to return the update.
  • Optional online URL or reputation checks may send the URL or domain you ask the app to verify, together with limited technical metadata needed for security, abuse prevention, and service operation.
  • Security systems may process pseudonymous device or installation identifiers to rate-limit abuse, protect the service, and enforce basic anti-fraud controls.

These network features are used to provide threat intelligence, keep the threat database current, and protect the service from abuse. They are not used to serve ads.

Payments and App Stores

If you purchase a subscription, the billing transaction is handled by the platform operator, such as Apple or Google. We do not receive your full payment card number.

We may receive store-side purchase status details that are necessary to unlock paid features, restore purchases, prevent fraud, and support subscription entitlement checks.

How We Use Data

Data processed through GlyphGuard may be used to:

  • run local and online security checks;
  • deliver threat database updates;
  • detect abuse, fraud, and service misuse;
  • maintain app reliability, security, and troubleshooting.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you remove it or uninstall the app.

Server-side logs and abuse-prevention records may be retained for a limited period as reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, debugging, and service operations.

Your Choices

You can use core offline analysis features without creating an account.

You can remove local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. If you contact us about a privacy request, include enough detail for us to understand which feature or request you are referring to.

Children's Privacy

GlyphGuard is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through the app.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the service and the limited data processed for app functionality, security, and abuse prevention. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy or data handling in GlyphGuard, contact us at glyphguard.app@gmail.com.
